Welcome to the Interkingdom Equestrian Competition Historical Website!

The Inter-Kingdom Equestrian Competition (IKEqC) is intended to be a Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA) wide competition in a grand tournament style, where all riders may compete honorably and appreciation of skill may overcome the boundaries of Kingdoms and limitations of travel. IKEqC is based on a similar premise to the Inter-Kingdom Archery Competition (IKAC).

Each tournament of the competition will run throughout the Anno-Societatus. That is to say, from May 1st through April 30th of the following calendar year.

This site displays historical scores for previous years in a format more suited for static web display.

This mirror does not maintain copies of the Rules or Scoresheet PDF files.
Please consult SCAIKEQC.ORG for the current versions.
